Usability

It is very important to practice usability in order to get a feedback of design in User Experience. The feedback allows you to go back and reﬁne part of the design to achieve ease of use. We perform all activities under our highly equipped lab.

Memorability: When users return to the design after a period of not using it, how easily can they re-establish proﬁciency?

Mistakes: How many mistakes do users make, how severe are these mistakes, and how easily can they recover from the mistakes?

Satisfaction: How pleasant is it to use the design?

Methods:
• Inspections Inquiry
• Prototyping and testing

We have a highly equipped lab for performing the usability Protocols.

Information Design

Information design is the practice of presenting information in a way that fosters eﬃcient and eﬀective understanding of it. The term has come to be used speciﬁcally for graphic design for displaying information eﬀectively, rather than just abstractively or for artistic expression. Today, information design is closely related to the ﬁeld of data visualization.
