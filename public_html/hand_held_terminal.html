Overview

Understanding the need to have an advanced mechanism, BBIPPL Handheld has introduced a Bus Ticketing Machine with sophisticated features and performance. This contemporary machine is used to give tickets to bus passengers. Additionally, the device can be connected to computer and download data and can receive various related reports from it.

The reports generated from Bus Ticketing Machine are:

  • Trip Sheet
  • Ticket Details
  • Stage Wise Reports
  • Concessions Reports
  • Refund Reports
  • Status Reports
  • Collection Reports
  • Toll Tax Reports
  • Night Out Reports
  • Erase Data Reports
