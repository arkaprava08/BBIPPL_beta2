Overview

21st Century Informatics (21 CI) provides Healthcare Informatics Solutions to healthcare service providers such as Hospitals, Diagnostic Centers, Specialty Clinics, etc. With over 1200+ man-years of domain expertise in healthcare processes and software systems, 21CI Healthcare Informatics Solutions have contributed to the success of 500+ healthcare organisations globally. 21 CI is headquartered in Amsterdam (The Netherlands), with its Global Development Center in Mumbai (India) and regional offices in UAE, Saudi Arabia and India. Our mission is to create software solutions for healthcare organisations, so as to enable the delivery of services to patients that are: - Affordable - Accessible - Accurate This has been our mission since we first started in 1995.
