Overview

Brick & Byte Innovative Products Pvt Ltd is a technology based engineering company having its own innovation centre. The patented “Indigenous LFR based Solar Thermal Technology “ was developed at the Brick and Byte Solar Research Centre. The company also has the State-Of –The –Art Design and Manufacturing plant where High Precision Solar Structures, Controllers, Inverters are Developed and Manufactured.

Under their Integrated Engineering Services division the Industrial Automation and Remote Monitoring solutions are developed and implemented for Solar Projects like rural electrification. Under Facility management Operation and Maintenance is done.

Innovation is done in collaboration with different institutes, Universities and Research Centers.
