Solar System for Water Pumping

Solar PV based System is specially developed for water pumping. It includes Pump Photovoltaic Panels, Controller, Special Structure and AC Motor. Controller is a combination of Inverter, MPPT and VFD. It uses the generated power from PV and converts in required form for AC Motor. Solar energy which is abundant on earth can be harnessed for this purpose.

  • This system works at its best during any time of the day at any kind of sunlight. The induction motor has very high starting current which is around four times of the running current of the motor. The VFD in controller eliminates the high starting current of the induction motor. Because of the smooth start using VFD, the motor will start as early as even 9am and works till 5:30pm. It delivers maximum water during 10am to 4pm.
  • Special Features for this system are:
    • NO BATTERY
    • NO STARTER
    • USE EXISTING AC MOTORS
    • 25 YEARS LIFE
    • SPARE PART AVAILABILITY
    • ONE TIME INVESTMENT
    • AUTO/MANUAL OPERATION
    • RANGE: 1HP to 50HP
  • Special Applications of this system are:
    • AGRICULTURE IRRIGATION
    • WASTEWATER TREATMENT
    • MUNCIPAL & CITY CENTRE SQUARES, PARKS
    • RESORTS, HOTELS & TOURIST SITES
