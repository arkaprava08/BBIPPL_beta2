ARUN K PANDE

Dr. Arun K. Pande currently offers Consultancy and Advisory services in the area of IT Innovation for Masses (ITIM). Prior to his own consultancy organization, he was Chief Scientist and Head of TCS Innovation Lab in Mumbai.

Dr. Pande has rich experience of thirty years in conducting research, technology development, deployment and management. Dr. Pande had been active in building technology teams and developing network with Academics and Research community through collaborative projects.

Dr. Pande holds a Ph. D. degree in Electrical Engineering from University of Notre Dame, USA. His current research and business interests are IT innovation for soil, water, crops, fisheries, livestock, primary healthcare and renewable energy. He is also interested in Wireless Data Application; Speech & Script technologies based applications for the masses. In last six years, he had been able to attract funds from Department of Science and Technology and International organizations like World Bank, IFAD (International Fund for Agriculture Development) to demonstrate impact of IT on Remote Speech Therapy, Climate Change Adaptation and Integrated Agriculture respectively.

Dr. Pande’s innovation at TCS on Mobile Based Advisory Services for the farmers has earned him several national and international awards.

Dr. Pande is a Senior Member of IEEE and served on the Industry Advisory Board of WCET programme of IEEE for three years.
