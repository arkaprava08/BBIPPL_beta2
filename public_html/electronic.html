Overview

BBIPPL is a full-service Electronic Manufacturing Services that designs, manufactures and services products for the computer, government, industrial, and medical and telecommunications markets. BBIPPL provides Electronic manufacturing services (EMS) for original equipment manufacturers (OEMs). With our contract manufacturing business unit, we transform your designs into rapid prototyping and enable them into large production runs. We add value to our clients by completely integrating their engineering work flows.
