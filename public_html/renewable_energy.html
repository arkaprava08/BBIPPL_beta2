Overview

Brick & Byte innovative Products Pvt Ltd is technology based engineering company having it’s own innovation centre. “Indigenous LFR based Solar Thermal Technology “ is developed in Brick and Byte Solar Research Centre and is patented. The company also has the State-Of –The –Art Design and Manufacturing plant where High Precision Solar Structures, Controllers, Inverters are Developed and Manufactured.

Under Integrated Engineering Services division the Industrial Automation, Remote Monitoring solutions are developed and implemented for Solar Projects like rural electrification.

Under Facility management Operation and Maintenance is done.

Innovation is done with collaboration of different institutes, Universities and Research Canters.
