Integrated Facility Management
Facility Management Challenges

Facilities are becoming highly complex and large structures. Some of the challenges faced by modern facilities include:

  • Security and Safety
    • How to provide comprehensive security and safety to facility and its users?
      • Terrorism
      • Crime
      • Fire
  • Operations Cost
    • How to reduce running cost of the facility?
    • How to monitor and optimize energy consumption?
      • Electricity
      • Water
      • Gas
  • Remote Management
    • How to manage facility from the remote/Internet?
  • Integration
    • How to integrate diverse range of operational and business systems used in a facility?
    • How to provide 360 Degree view of the facility from the same work station?

Facility Policy and Process Use Cases

Emergency policy

  • Emergency Drill
    • Perform periodic (Configurable) emergency drill
  • Emergency Detection/Declaration
    • System detect the emergency type and level ( Fire, Perimeter violation etc.)
    • User declares emergency type and level ( Fire, Terrorist attack etc.)
  • Emergency Response
    • During real emergency or mock emergency drill, all exits , including emergency exits, should be opened
    • Power to the lifts should be cut off (During fire)
    • Public address system should broadcast emergency situation guidelines
    • Depending upon emergency type and level , notify following parties
      • Fire station
      • Ambulance
      • Hospitals
    • User should be able to escalate the emergency level (Low, Medium and High)

Asset Maintenance

  • Perform periodic (configurable) maintenance of assets
    • Lifts and escalators
    • Generator sets
    • Air Conditioning systems
    • Pump House
  • Manage the work order lifecycle for asset maintenance

Energy optimization

  • Real time Monitoring of all energy resources
    • Electricity
    • Water
    • Gas
  • Set AC and light setting based upon number of persons

In case of fire in a department/section:

  • Disable access control for that area
  • Highlight the relevant camera feed in the LCD
  • Alert the relevant security personnel by Email, SMS or Phone
