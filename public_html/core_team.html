Narayan Kunté is the Senior Vice President of Brick and Byte Technologies Inc. USA, and Brick and Byte Innovative products Pvt. Ltd. Narayan’s leadership experience includes Corporate Governance, Human Capital Management, Customer Relationship Management, Business Development Marketing and Organization Strategy.

Before joining Brick and Byte he worked with Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), Computer Science Corporation (CSC), Syntel Inc, Sigma Systems Inc, and Capgemini Ltd India, to name a few. As part of his current role, he is responsible for building the brand and leading the organization by achievement.

Like all executive team leaders in Brick and Byte, Narayan’s work plays a crucial role in serving for Brick and Byte's professional investors and protecting the Brick and Byte brand for its product and services, while providing high quality service to their largest and most valued clients. Narayan intends to build a team and hire the best people in the industry for future success.

Outside Brick and Byte, Narayan balances his career and family life, wife and two talented daughters. Committed to helping others, he actively supports local cultural and social organizations and served in India Cultural Society of Rockland (ICSR) as a committee member for many years. He earned his Master’s degree M.B.A in Marketing Management from Symbiosis Institute of Business Management with University of Pune, India.
